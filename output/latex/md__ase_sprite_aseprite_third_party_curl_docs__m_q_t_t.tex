\hypertarget{md__ase_sprite_aseprite_third_party_curl_docs__m_q_t_t_autotoc_md1137}{}\doxysection{Usage}\label{md__ase_sprite_aseprite_third_party_curl_docs__m_q_t_t_autotoc_md1137}
\mbox{\hyperlink{class_a}{A}} plain \char`\"{}\+GET\char`\"{} subscribes to the topic and prints all published messages. Doing a \char`\"{}\+POST\char`\"{} publishes the post data to the topic and exits.

Example subscribe\+: \begin{DoxyVerb}curl mqtt://host/home/bedroom/temp
\end{DoxyVerb}
 Example publish\+: \begin{DoxyVerb}curl -d 75 mqtt://host/home/bedroom/dimmer
\end{DoxyVerb}
